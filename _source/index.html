Announcing foss-north 2020

foss-north 2020 will run from March 29 - April 1 in Gothenburg, Sweden. It will consist of one community day filled with workshops, unconferences, hackathons, and fun, then two conference days, and end with a day of training sessions. We are currently looking for sponsors!

 

Speakers

We are looking for speakers. Do you want to meet our community? Join our Call for Papers!

We are pleased to present the following speakers for the 2020 event:

 

Anne-Marie Eklund-Löwinder
Chief Information Officer at The Swedish Internet Foundation

Frank Karlitschek
Founder of NextCloud
